## Overview

Haldeki Market is an innovative e-commerce platform that connects wholesale produce markets (hal) directly to customers in İzmir, Turkey. The platform eliminates traditional retail markups by sourcing fresh fruits and vegetables from wholesalers and delivering them to homes and businesses on the same day.

## Business Model

**Direct-to-Consumer Model:**
- Sources products from wholesale markets (hal)
- Cuts out middlemen (retailers, supermarkets)
- Passes savings directly to customers
- Same-day delivery from market to door

**Target Customers:**
- Households in Menemen and Aliağa regions
- Restaurants and cafes (B2B)
- Hotels and hospitality businesses
- Institutional buyers

## Service Areas

**Primary Coverage:**
1. **Menemen, İzmir**
   - Central delivery hub
   - Same-day delivery available
   - Free delivery over 150 TL

2. **Aliağa, İzmir**
   - Same-day delivery available
   - Free delivery over 150 TL
   - Industrial zone coverage

## Product Categories

**Fresh Produce:**
- Vegetables (Sebzeler): Tomatoes, cucumbers, peppers, eggplant, onions, potatoes, leafy greens
- Fruits (Meyveler): Citrus, apples, pears, stone fruits, seasonal fruits
- Herbs (Yeşillikler): Parsley, dill, mint, cilantro, green onions

**Quality Tiers:**
- **Premium (Özenle Seçilmiş):** Hand-selected, highest quality
- **Standard (Standart):** Regular quality, everyday pricing
- **Bugün Halde (Today's Special):** Discounted items, limited quantity
