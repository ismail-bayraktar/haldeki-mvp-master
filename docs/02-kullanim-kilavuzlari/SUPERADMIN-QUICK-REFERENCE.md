## Quick Actions

### Create User
1. Business Panel → Users
2. Click "Create User"
3. Fill in details
4. Click "Create"

### Approve Supplier
1. Business Panel → Suppliers
2. Find pending supplier
3. Click "Approve"

### View All Products
1. Business Panel → Products
2. Filter by supplier/category

### View All Orders
1. Business Panel → Orders
2. Filter by status/date
